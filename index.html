Rock, Paper, Scissors

How to Play:

  • Click on one of the buttons: Rock, Paper, or Scissors.
  • The computer will randomly select one of the three options.
  • The winner is determined based on the rules below:
    • Rock beats Scissors
    • Scissors beats Paper
    • Paper beats Rock

The first player to reach 5 points wins the game!
